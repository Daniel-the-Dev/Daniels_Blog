Font Structure

When using HTML we can format our text. Tags such as "strong" , "em", and "u" are all ways we style out text.
(strong) makes our font Bold
(em) makes our font Italics
(u) gives our font an Underline
Personal Preferance: Make sure to keep your tags lowercase. Makes everything look cleaner.
